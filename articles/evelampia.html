Elevating family business with fresh look

In early 2025, I was approached by my mother. She is an artist who draws oil paintings for a living, and she asked me to create thank-you cards to include with her paintings. What started as a small request turned into a larger project.
Over the next half year, I cooperated with her to build a more defined visual system around her work. We discussed what she wanted to communicate to her buyers, explored different directions, and tested several ideas together. The goal was to create something that felt personal to her, but also consistent and clear. During this process, I worked on layouts, selected type, and introduced small graphic elements that matched the tone of her paintings. She contributed sketches and rough concepts, and we combined both approaches into a simple system that she could use for her cards and other small printed materials. By the end, we arrived at a set of thank-you cards and a basic visual identity that felt natural to her style and helped present her work in a more intentional way.
